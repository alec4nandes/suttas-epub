1. Five Lower Fetters
  1. identity view
  2. doubt
  3. misapprehension of precepts and observances
  4. sensual desire
  5. ill will
Five Higher Fetters
  1. desire for rebirth in the realm of luminous form
  2. desire for rebirth in the formless realm
  3. conceit
  4. restlessness
  5. ignorance
